# WU Redistribute Control

**Colors:** White/Blue
**Archetype:** Control
**Primary Mechanic:** REDISTRIBUTE
**Secondary Theme:** Counter manipulation, Defensive play

---

## Deck Strategy

This deck tests the **REDISTRIBUTE mechanic** by building around counter equalization as a control tool. The strategy is to:

1. Let opponents build up counters on their permanents
2. Use redistribute to "tax" their counters (rounding down = net loss)
3. Spread counters to your own permanents for value
4. Control the game through efficient redistribution timing

**Key Interaction:** Redistribute always results in net counter loss due to rounding down. This makes it a form of removal against counter-based threats.
