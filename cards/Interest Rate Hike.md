## Design Notes
- Red uncommon instant
- Mass debt increase: every Debt now deals +2 damage
- Devastating when multiple Debts are in play (3 Debts = +6 total damage)
- Red's chaotic, destructive economics
- Cross-format: Works with ANY Debt tokens in any format
- Pairs with: [[Compound Interest]], [[Default Cascade]]

## Outside of this set interactions

- **Potential Combos:** If Debt tokens deal damage equal to debt counters, mass Debt + this = massive damage spike
- **Balance Assessment:** Fair - Requires Debt tokens to exist. Set-specific amplifier.
