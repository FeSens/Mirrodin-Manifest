# Spite Tax

## Card Type Line
Instant

## Rules Text
> Choose two Debt tokens and redistribute them. Target opponent loses 2 life. *(To redistribute, remove all counters from them, then each gets half that many counters, rounded down.)*

## Flavor Text
> *"Your debts are my pleasure."*

## Image Prompt
> Spite Tax — Instant. Action depicted: Debt tokens being forcibly redistributed between two victims while one collapses from sudden life drain. Setting: Vault of Whispers tax collection office, chrome desks and dark corners. Focal point: The moment debt counters leap between targets as one victim withers. Mood: Corrupted triumph. Style reference: Nils Hamm.
