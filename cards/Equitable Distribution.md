# Equitable Distribution

## Card Type Line
Sorcery

## Rules Text
> Choose two target permanents with counters on them. Redistribute among them. *(To redistribute, remove all counters from them, then each gets half that many counters, rounded down.)*

## Flavor Text
> *"All shall be made equal under the Synod's watchful eye."*

## Image Prompt
> Equitable Distribution — Sorcery. Action depicted: Two creatures of vastly different sizes having their power visibly averaged — energy flowing between them to equalize. Setting: The Great Furnace/Mer-Ikan Foundry — Synod enforcement zone. Focal point: The energy transfer between largest and smallest creature. Mood: Institutional horror. Style reference: Seb McKinnon meets Greg Staples. Color palette: White energy flow, gold, bronze creatures.

## Design Notes
- White common sorcery - addresses REDISTRIBUTE common gap
- [[☭REDISTRIBUTE]] synergy at common
- Simple, flavorful effect for Limited
