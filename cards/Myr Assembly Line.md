## Design Notes
- Colorless uncommon for Myr tribal
- Repeatable token generation

- Pairs with: [[Myr Replicant]], [[Simulated Labor Myr]], [[The Myr Collective]]

## Outside of this set interactions
- **Notable Synergies:** [[Intruder Alarm]] (won't go infinite alone due to sorcery restriction), [[Doubling Season]] / [[Parallel Lives]] (double tokens), [[Clock of Omens]] (untap for more activations but not infinite without more pieces), [[Krark-Clan Ironworks]] (sacrifice Myr for mana)
- **Balance Assessment:** Fair - The sorcery-speed restriction is key. Without it, this would be broken with Intruder Alarm. As-is, it's appropriately powered.
