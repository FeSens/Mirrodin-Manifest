# Den of Fortune

## Card Type Line
Land

## Rules Text
> {T}: Add {C}.
>
> {T}: Add {R}. Spend this mana only to cast spells with gamble or spells that cause you to gamble.
>
> {2}{R}, {T}: Gamble. If you win, add {R}{R}{R}. If you lose, Den of Fortune deals 2 damage to you.

## Flavor Text
> *"The house always wins. Unless you ARE the house."*

## Image Prompt
> A hidden gambling den in the Glimmervoid, tables surrounded by desperate players. Gold and danger in equal measure.

## Design Notes
- **Gamble archetype land** - mana support for gamble decks
- Connects: [[All-In Gambler]], [[Gambling with the Devil]], [[Gambler's Instinct]], [[Lucky Strike]], [[Reckless Gambit]], [[Jackpot Wurm]], [[Tangle Titan]]
- Mana for gamble spells + gamble activation
- Risk/reward mana ability
- Pairs with: [[Den of Chance]], [[Fortune's Favor]], [[The House Edge]], [[Calculated Risk]]
- Enables dedicated Gamble archetype
