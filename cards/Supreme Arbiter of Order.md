## Outside of this set interactions
- **Notable Synergies:** Devastating with [[Winter Orb]], [[Static Orb]], [[Stasis]] - opponents can barely untap; [[Smothering Tithe]] generates Treasures while opponents struggle; [[Blind Obedience]] ensures permanents enter tapped
- **POTENTIALLY BROKEN Combos:** [[Stasis]] + this = opponents keep 1 permanent untapped, but Stasis prevents even that from untapping; [[Winter Orb]] + this = opponents effectively can't function; [[Kismet]]/[[Frozen Aether]] + this = permanents enter tapped and mostly stay tapped
- **Balance Assessment:** POTENTIALLY BROKEN - Creates near-complete lock with common stax pieces. Opponents keeping only ONE untapped nonland permanent is extremely restrictive. Consider changing to "tap up to two" or limiting to creatures only
