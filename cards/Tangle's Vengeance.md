# Tangle's Vengeance

## Card Type Line
Enchantment

## Rules Text
> Elf creatures you control get +1/+1.
>
> Whenever an Elf enters the battlefield under your control, you may have it fight target creature an opponent controls with power 2 or less.
>
> {3}{G}: Create a 2/2 green Elf Warrior creature token.

## Flavor Text
> *"The copper trees remember. The Tangle never forgives."*

## Image Prompt
> Ancient Elf warriors emerging from the twisted metal forest, vines and copper branches reaching out to entangle Foundry invaders.

## Design Notes
- **Elf/Tangle tribal hub** - connects all Tangle cards
- Connects: [[Tangle Ambusher]], [[Tangle Titan]], [[Tangle Reclaimer]], [[Copper Myr Tender]], [[Tel-Jilad Defender]], [[Overgrown Sentinel]]
- Fight trigger represents the Tangle's resistance
- Token generation for board presence
- Lore connection: [[Lore#The Tangle Resistance]]
- Pairs with: [[Hidden Community Elder]], [[Quicksilver Passage Guide]], [[Sanctuary Manifest]]
