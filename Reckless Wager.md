# Reckless Wager

## Card Type Line
Sorcery

## Rules Text
> Draw two cards, then discard a card.
>
> Gamble. If you lose the gamble, discard another card.

## Flavor Text
> *"Double or nothing? I'll take those odds."*

## Image Prompt
> A human gambler at a Glimmervoid table, sweating as they draw cards from a chrome deck. Gold tokens pile high, but their hand trembles.

- Red common sorcery - addresses Red common gap (-16)
- Addresses common creature ratio issue

**Format Assessment:**
- **Commander**: Decent card filtering; gamble adds variance that some playgroups enjoy
- **Modern/Legacy**: Compares to Faithless Looting (banned) but with downside variance
- **Limited**: Strong common - floor is still draw 2 discard 2, ceiling is draw 2 discard 1
