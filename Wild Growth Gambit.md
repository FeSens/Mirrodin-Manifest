# Wild Growth Gambit

## Card Type Line
Sorcery

## Rules Text
> Gamble. If you win the gamble, search your library for a basic land card, put it onto the battlefield tapped, then shuffle. If you lose, search your library for a basic land card, put it into your hand, then shuffle.

## Flavor Text
> *"Nature rewards the bold. Or at least, the lucky."*

## Image Prompt
> A Sylvok druid holding glowing seeds, some sprouting into massive trees, others wilting. The Tangle's growth is unpredictable.

**Format Assessment:**
- **Commander**: Excellent - floor is Lay of the Land, ceiling is Rampant Growth
- **Modern/Legacy**: Compares favorably to other 2-mana ramp with upside variance
- **Limited**: Premium common - ramp is always valuable, and floor is still playable
