## üîÅ Why Compound Is Not Proliferate

|Proliferate|Compound|
|---|---|

# üß† **The Mechanical Triangle (Set Identity)**

|Mechanic|Philosophy|Gameplay Effect|
|---|---|---|
|**Gamble**|Risk & volatility|Creates variance|
|**Compound**|Capital accumulation|Amplifies inequality|
|**Redistribute**|Forced equality|Destroys efficiency|

This triangle is **exceptionally strong set design**.  
Each mechanic:

- Counters the others
    
- Creates political tension
    
- Teaches ideology _through gameplay_
