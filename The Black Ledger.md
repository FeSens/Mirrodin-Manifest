# The Black Ledger

## Card Type Line
Legendary Artifact

## Rules Text
> {T}: Look at the top card of target opponent's library. You may have that player reveal it.
>
> {2}, {T}: Name a card. Target opponent reveals their hand. If the named card is in their hand, that player discards it and you draw a card.
>
> {4}, {T}, Sacrifice The Black Ledger: Each opponent reveals their hand and library. You may cast a legendary spell from among those cards without paying its mana cost. Then each opponent shuffles.
